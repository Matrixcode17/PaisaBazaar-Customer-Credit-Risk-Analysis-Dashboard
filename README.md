# PaisaBazaar Customer Credit Risk Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of customer credit risk by examining credit scores, outstanding debt, income levels, payment behavior, and credit utilization patterns. The dashboard helps financial institutions and lending organizations identify risk segments, monitor customer credit health, and support data-driven lending decisions.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to analyze customer creditworthiness and identify potential risk factors that may impact lending decisions. By visualizing key credit metrics and customer behavior patterns, stakeholders can:

* Assess overall portfolio risk.
* Identify high-risk customer segments.
* Monitor debt and payment performance.
* Evaluate relationships between income, debt, and credit scores.
* Support credit approval and risk management strategies.

## Visualizations

### 1. Credit Score Distribution

Displays the proportion of customers across different credit score categories, enabling quick identification of dominant customer segments.

### 2. Delayed Payments by Credit Score

Compares average delayed payment behavior across credit score groups, highlighting payment risk trends.

### 3. Outstanding Debt by Credit Score

Analyzes total debt exposure for each credit score segment to identify high-risk categories.

### 4. Outstanding Debt vs Annual Income

Scatter plot visualization showing the relationship between customer income and debt levels, segmented by credit score.

### 5. Credit History Age by Credit Score

Measures average credit history length across customer segments to understand credit maturity patterns.

### 6. Customers by Credit Mix and Credit Score

Provides a comparative view of customer distribution across credit mix categories and credit score classifications.

---

## Key Insights

* Customers with poor credit scores tend to exhibit higher delayed payment behavior.
* Outstanding debt levels vary significantly across credit score categories.
* Longer credit histories are generally associated with better credit performance.
* Credit utilization and debt metrics provide valuable indicators for assessing customer risk.
* Income and debt relationships help identify customers with potential repayment concerns.
